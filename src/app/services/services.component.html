Services

Phone Banking Our experienced volunteers will phone bank for our endorsed candidates at least three times during the course of a campaign. Block Walking We will set up targeted block-walks for our endorsed candidates (location permitting) Data Entry Data collection is imperative to a successful campaign, volunteers will enter data for at least eight collective hours for our endorsed candidates. Social Media Marketing Any activity done on behalf of an endorsed candidate will be put on multiple social media platforms utilizing the hashtag of that particular campaign.
