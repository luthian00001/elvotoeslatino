El Voto Es Latino

El Voto Es Latino

EL VOTO ES LATINO is an unincorporated Political Action Committee, which simply means we are not a big corporation. We are a small group with one goal: support candidates who support people.

EL VOTO ES LATINO promotes the improvement of campaign engagement with the Latino community by encouraging our membership to take an active part in influencing governmental affairs through political action.

EL VOTO ES LATINO is a voluntary, non-profit, unincorporated committee operated in conformance with state and federal law.

EL VOTO ES LATINO believes that when people come together to create change, change will happen!

EL VOTO ES LATINO believes in a government of, by, and for the people!

El Voto Es Latino Educados

El Voto Es Latino Educados

El Voto Es Latino Educados's primary goal is to bridge the gap between Latinos and the ballot box.

One of the primary ways we do this is through education on the voting and electoral process

Some of the topics we address:

  • how to register
  • how to vote
  • upcoming elections
  • the duties of elected officials
  • current voting requirements
